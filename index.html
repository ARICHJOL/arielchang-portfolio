About Me

Creative strategist.
Human-centered designer.

I bring a unique blend of creativity, strategy, and technical expertise — enhanced by AI‑enabled workflows — to every learning project. My journey spans roles from technical trainer to senior instructional designer, collaborating with cross‑functional teams to deliver high‑impact, data‑informed learning solutions.

I design and develop engaging eLearning experiences using Articulate 360 and Rise, accelerated by AI for rapid prototyping, content ideation, and iterative refinement. I also facilitate scenario‑based training and onboarding programs, lead performance improvement initiatives, and manage end-to-end learning projects with multiple stakeholders.

With fluency in English and Filipino and experience across diverse industries, I design content that connects across cultures and contexts. Whether you're a hiring manager, collaborator, or fellow designer — I invite you to explore how I turn ideas into impactful learning experiences.
